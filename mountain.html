Mountain Retreat

Breathe in the fresh mountain air, treat your eyes to the spectacular views and lose yourself in the charming ambience to feel refreshed from within. Mountains offer a variety of activities, including skiing, snowboarding, mountain biking, paragliding, walking tours, and more.

“Adventure awaits where the mountains meet the sky.” “In the mountains, you feel the majesty of the earth and the presence of the divine.”

Explore the world of mountains with us!

Kasol

The hill-hamlet of Kasol, in Himachal Pradesh, and flaunts raw natural beauty with lush conifer forests, snow-capped mountains and winding rivers

Lansdowne

Untouched by urbanization, Lansdowne is a quaint and charming hill station with oak forests, snow-capped mountains and lush green valleys.

Dalhousie

Located in the lush green surroundings of Chamba district in Himachal Pradesh, Dalhousie is a serene town that still has its natural beauty well preserved.

Shimla

The erstwhile British ‘Summer Capital’ is packed with colonial-era architectural marvels, picturesque hilly hotspots.

Home to glittering lakes, alpine pastures, crystal clear mountain streams and stunning views of Alps wherever you look.

Malaysia is the perfect amalgamation of the buzzing cosmopolitan life and the serene pristine beaches. The country enjoys a seamless mix of cultures.

Sri Lanka
