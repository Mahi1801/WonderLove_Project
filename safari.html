City Break

Want to explore some of the most sought-after city breaks and beyond? From bustling metropolises to charming small towns, city breaks cater to every traveler's taste whether you're a history buff, foodie, or adventure seeker. Travel to different places during holidays for fun and relaxation. While others do it to experience the culture, enjoy the sceneries.

"A vacation is having nothing to do and all day to do it in" Take only memories, leave only footprints.

Explore the world of cities with us!

Kasol

The hill-hamlet of Kasol, in Himachal Pradesh, and flaunts raw natural beauty with lush conifer forests, snow-capped mountains and winding rivers

Lansdowne

Untouched by urbanization, Lansdowne is a quaint and charming hill station with oak forests, snow-capped mountains and lush green valleys.

Dalhousie

Located in the lush green surroundings of Chamba district in Himachal Pradesh, Dalhousie is a serene town that still has its natural beauty well preserved.

Thailand

From beaches and temples to shopping, food and spas; Thailand is adorned with a stunning coastline, magnificent hills and spectacular sceneries.

Malaysia

Malaysia is the perfect amalgamation of the buzzing cosmopolitan life and the serene pristine beaches. The country enjoys a seamless mix of cultures.

Sri Lanka

The teardrop-shaped island while the golden beaches and azure waters line the coast, the verdant hills and cascading waterfalls dot the inland areas.

Shimla

The erstwhile British ‘Summer Capital’ is packed with colonial-era architectural marvels, picturesque hilly hotspots.

Switzerland

Home to glittering lakes, alpine pastures, crystal clear mountain streams and stunning views of Alps wherever you look.

Japan

xceedingly modern with a culture that is still lodged in the old, traditional ways, Japan impresses you with its ethos, rock gardens.
