City Break

Want to explore some of the most sought-after city breaks and beyond? From bustling metropolises to charming small towns, city breaks cater to every traveler's taste whether you're a history buff, foodie, or adventure seeker. Travel to different places during holidays for fun and relaxation. While others do it to experience the culture, enjoy the sceneries.

"A vacation is having nothing to do and all day to do it in" Take only memories, leave only footprints.

Explore the world of cities with us!

Los Angeles

Known all over the world as the home of Hollywood and its glittering movie stars, LA is the hub of creativity and legends. It has natural beauty.

Singapore

A melting pot of cultures, Singapore is a cosmopolitan city with a vibrant shopping, food, entertainment and nightlife scene.

Australia

Visit architectural marvels or perhaps walk in the footsteps of the earliest humans, Australia promises to offer many magical experiences.

China

China shines bright on the world map with its deep-rooted culture, scenic beauty and out-of-the-world shopping experiences.

The exotic country is known for its beaches, volcanoes, komodo dragons, and jungles sheltering elephants and tigers.

USA

United Kingdom

Known for its historical landmarks, picturesque landscapes, and vibrant cities, the UK offers a multitude of experiences for travellers.

France

From the romantically captivating Paris to the picturesque landscapes of Provence, France is a haven for globetrotters.

South Korea

South Korea known for its technological advancements, vibrant pop culture, Buddhist temples, and rich culinary heritage.
